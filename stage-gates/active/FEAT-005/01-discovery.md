# FEAT-005 Discovery

## Goal

Define a deterministic water-only bench procedure for leak, pressure, e-stop, low-liquid, zone-valve, catch-cup, and fault-safe checks before fertilizer or chemical tests.

## Inputs

- FEAT-004 bench ratings contract.
- FEAT-003 hardware wiring and safety boundary.
- Route-defined spraying requirement: LEFT, RIGHT, BOTH, OFF.

## Constraints

- Water-only validation first.
- No bypassing e-stop, low-liquid, or pressure interlocks.
- No physical test data is fabricated; this feature defines the procedure and log template only.
